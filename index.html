The Challenge of Contamination

Incorrect sorting of items like **plastic bags, greasy pizza boxes, or Styrofoam** forces city workers to manually sort or reject entire batches.

This contamination **increases costs** for the city and **reduces our positive environmental impact.**

Let's work together to make Hartselle's recycling process efficient!

About This Project

This assistant is powered by an Image Classification model (envisioned using **Google's Teachable Machine**) trained to visually identify different waste materials.

Our goal is to improve communication and empower every Hartselle resident to sort their waste **correctly** and be better environmental stewards.
